querying -> 

db.test_collection.find({});

update command

db.test_collection.updateMany({ age: 10 }, {$set: {"id":1}})


how to delete a document 
db.test_collection.deleteOne({ age: 10 });


to delete multiple documetns;

db.test_collection.deleteMany({ age: 10 });

db.test_collection.countDocuments({ age: 10 });
